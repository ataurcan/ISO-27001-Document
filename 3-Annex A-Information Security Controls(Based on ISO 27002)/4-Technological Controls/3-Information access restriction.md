
**Control** 
    Access to information and other associated assets should be restricted in accordance with the established topic-specific policy on access control.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203152551.png)

**Purpose** 
 To ensure only authorized access and to prevent unauthorized access to information and other associated assets.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use access control lists and permissions. 
- Apply least privilege and need-to-know principles.
- Review access regularly.

**Other Information** 
    Supports compliance with confidentiality policies.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Role-based access is often misconfigured or too broad, violating least privilege. 
- Access review processes are manual and inconsistently applied.
- Excessive access privileges accumulate over time due to lack of cleanup.
