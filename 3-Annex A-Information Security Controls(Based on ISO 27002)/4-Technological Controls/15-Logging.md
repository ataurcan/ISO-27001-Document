
**Control** 
    Logs that record activities, exceptions, faults and other relevant events should be produced, stored, protected and analysed.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203170733.png)


**Purpose** 
    To record events, generate evidence, ensure the integrity of log information, prevent against unauthorized access, identify information security events that can lead to an information security incident and to support investigations.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- sufficient detail for accountability.
- Protect log integrity and confidentiality. 
- Retain logs in line with policies.

**Other Information** 
    Ensure logs are regularly reviewed.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Logs may lack sufficient detail or timestamp accuracy for investigations.
- Storage and protection of logs are often neglected. 
- Regular review and analysis of logs is rarely done effectively.
