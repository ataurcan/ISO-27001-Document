
**Control** 
    Backup copies of information, software and systems should be maintained and regularly tested in accordance with the agreed topic-specific policy on backup.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203165900.png)

**Purpose** 
    To enable recovery from loss of data or systems.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Schedule backups based on criticality. 
- Store backups securely, off-site if necessary. 
- Test restoration periodically.

**Other Information** 
    Document backup and recovery responsibilities.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Backup schedules may not align with business-criticality or RTO/RPO. 
- Restoration procedures are rarely tested or documented. 
- Off-site or cloud backups may lack proper encryption and access controls.
