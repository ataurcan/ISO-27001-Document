
**Control** 
    Access to external websites should be managed to reduce exposure to malicious content.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203175315.png)


**Purpose** 
    To protect systems from being compromised by malware and to prevent access to unauthorized web resources.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use URL filtering and category-based blocking. 
- Monitor browsing activity and adjust rules as needed. 
- Inform users of acceptable use policies.

**Other Information**
    Helps prevent malware infections and data leakage.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Web filters can produce false positives, leading to user complaints and workarounds.
- Acceptable use policies are often not communicated clearly to users. 
- Monitoring of browsing activity is seen as invasive and hard to justify.
