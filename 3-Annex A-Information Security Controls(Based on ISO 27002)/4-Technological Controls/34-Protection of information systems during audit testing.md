
**Control** 
    Audit tests and other assurance activities involving assessment of operational systems should be planned and agreed between the tester and appropriate management.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203182829.png)


**Purpose** 
    To minimize the impact of audit and other assurance activities on operational systems and business processes.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Plan tests to minimize impact on operations.
- Use qualified personnel for tests and audits.
- Authorize and monitor test activities.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Uncoordinated testing can disrupt live systems or cause data loss.
- Testers may lack proper authorization or operate without oversight. 
- Audit/test activities are not always logged or reviewed for impact.
