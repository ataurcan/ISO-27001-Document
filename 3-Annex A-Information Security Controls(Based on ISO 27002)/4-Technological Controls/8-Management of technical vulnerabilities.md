
**Control** 
    Information about technical vulnerabilities of information systems in use should be obtained, the organization's exposure to such vulnerabilities should be evaluated and appropriate measures should be taken.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203164404.png)

**Purpose**
    To prevent exploitation of technical vulnerabilities.


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Monitor sources for vulnerability information.
- Apply patches and updates promptly. 
- Keep asset inventory up to date.

**Other Information**
    Use risk-based approach for prioritization.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Vulnerability scanning is irregular or limited in scope.
- Patching is delayed due to poor coordination with operations. 
- Risk-based prioritization of vulnerabilities is inconsistently applied.
