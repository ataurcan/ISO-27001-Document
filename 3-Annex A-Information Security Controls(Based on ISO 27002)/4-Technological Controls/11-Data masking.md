
**Control** 
    Data masking should be used in accordance with the organization's topic-specific policy on access control and other related topic-specific policies, and business requirements, taking applicable legislation into consideration.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203165331.png)


**Purpose** 
    To limit the exposure of sensitive data including PIl, and to comply with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Apply masking to personal and sensitive data. 
- Use anonymization or pseudonymization where feasible. 
- Control access to unmasked data.

**Other Information** 
    Supports privacy and compliance requirements.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Misuse of masking techniques (e.g., partial masking) can expose sensitive data. 
- Lack of clear masking standards across environments (e.g., dev/test vs. prod).
- Unmasked access is often poorly controlled or logged.
