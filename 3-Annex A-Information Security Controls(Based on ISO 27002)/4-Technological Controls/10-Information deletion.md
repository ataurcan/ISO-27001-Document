
**Control** 
    Information stored in information systems, devices or in any other storage media should be deleted hen no longer required.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203164941.png)

**Purpose** 
    To prevent unnecessary exposure of sensitive information and to comply with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements for information deletion.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use overwriting, degaussing or physical destruction.
- Apply policies for regular review of data retention. 
- Document deletion processes.

**Other Information** 
    Align with legal and business requirements.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Secure deletion methods are inconsistently applied across systems. 
- Data retention periods are not well-defined or enforced. 
- Deletion logs and verification are often missing or incomplete.
