
**Control** 
    Procedures and measures should be implemented to securely manage software installation on operational systems.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203174024.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure the integrity of operational systems and prevent exploitation of technical vulnerabilities. 

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use approval and testing processes before installation.
- Restrict installation rights to authorized staff. 
- Maintain software inventory.

**Other Information** 
    Align with configuration and change management.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Unauthorized software installations often occur due to lack of enforcement. 
- Testing and approval processes are either missing or bypassed. 
- Software inventories are outdated or incomplete.
