
**Control** 
    The use of resources should be monitored and adjusted in line with current and expected capacity requirements.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203153835.png)

**Purpose**
    To ensure the required capacity of information processing facilities, human resources, offices and other facilities.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Forecast future capacity needs. 
- Monitor usage trends and thresholds. 
- Plan upgrades or optimizations proactively.

**Other Information** 
    Supports business continuity and performance.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Capacity planning is reactive rather than predictive. 
- Monitoring tools are either lacking or poorly configured. 
- Lack of ownership or defined responsibilities delays capacity upgrades.
