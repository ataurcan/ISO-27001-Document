
**Control**
    Security measures for working in secure areas should be designed and implemented.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203130944.png)

**Purpose** 
    To protect information and other associated assets in secure areas from damage and unauthorized interference by personnel working in these areas.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Define rules for personnel behavior. 
- Restrict use of electronic devices.
- Supervise maintenance and cleaning staff.

**Other Information** 
    Visitors must be authorized and monitored

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Many organizations underinvest in physical protections like fire suppression. 
- Environmental threats (e.g., water leaks, heat) are not always part of risk assessments. 
- Testing of protective systems is irregular or undocumented.
