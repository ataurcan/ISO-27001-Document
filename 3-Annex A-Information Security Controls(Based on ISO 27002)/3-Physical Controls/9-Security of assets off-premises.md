
**Control**
    Off-site assets should be protected.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203132223.png)


**Purpose** 
    To prevent loss, damage, theft or compromise of off-site devices and interruption to the organization's operations.

**Guidance (Summary)**
- Use encryption and secure storage. 
- Apply clear procedures for transporting equipment. 
- Monitor asset location and usage.

**Other Information** 
    Applies to mobile devices and home working.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Tracking and securing mobile assets (e.g., laptops, USBs) offsite is challenging. 
- Employees may use personal devices without appropriate controls. 
- Security policies for remote or travel use are often unenforced.
