
**Control** 
    Items of equipment containing storage media should be verified to ensure that any sensitive data and licensed software has been removed or securely overwritten prior to disposal or re-use.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203150638.png)

**Purpose** 
    To prevent leakage of information from equipment to be disposed or re-used.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use secure erasure or physical destruction methods.
- Verify data removal before disposal or re-use. 
- Document and track disposal activities.

**Other Information** 
    Include leased or third-party returned equipment.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Data is not always fully erased before equipment is disposed or repurposed.
- Disposal tracking is often incomplete or undocumented.
- Reused equipment may bypass re-validation or reconfiguration steps.
