
**Control** 
    Storage media should be managed through their life cycle of acquisition, use, transportation and disposal in accordance with the organization's classification scheme and handling requirements.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203132703.png)


**Purpose**
    To ensure only authorized disclosure, modification, removal or destruction of information on storage

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use secure storage for sensitive media. 
- Control access and track media movements. 
- Use secure disposal methods when media is no longer needed.

**Other Information**
    Includes backup tapes, USB drives and optical media.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Movement and handling of storage media are not always logged or monitored. 
- Secure disposal methods are often bypassed due to convenience. 
- Encryption and access controls for portable media are inconsistently applied.
