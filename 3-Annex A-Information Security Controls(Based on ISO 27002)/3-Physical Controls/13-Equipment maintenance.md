
**Control** 
    Equipment should be maintained correctly to ensure availability, integrity and confidentiality of information. 

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203150102.png)

**Purpose** 
    To prevent loss, damage, theft or compromise of information and other associated assets and interruption to the organization's operations caused by lack of maintenance.


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Follow manufacturer and internal maintenance schedules. 
- Authorize and monitor third-party maintenance providers. 
- Verify proper data protection during repairs.

**Other Information** 
    Log maintenance activities.


**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Maintenance activities are performed without proper logging or oversight. 
- Third-party technicians may not follow data protection protocols. 
- Downtime and risks during maintenance are not well-managed.
