
**Control** 
    Information processing facilities should be protected from power failures and other disruptions caused by failures in supporting utilities.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203145006.png)


**Purpose** 
    To prevent loss, damage or compromise of information and other associated assets, or interruption to the organization's operations due to failure and disruption of supporting utilities.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use backup power supplies and surge protection. 
- Protect utility lines from physical damage.
- Monitor utility performance and security.

**Other Information** 
    Supports system uptime and resilience.


**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Backup utilities like UPS and generators are not always tested regularly. 
- Utility protection measures may lack physical shielding or redundancy.
- Disruptions in power or telecoms can severely impact system availability.
