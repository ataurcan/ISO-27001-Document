
**Control** 
    Equipment should be sited securely and protected.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203131807.png)


**Purpose** 
    To reduce the risks from physical and environmental threats, and from unauthorized access and damage.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Locate equipment to avoid physical hazards. 
- Use locks and secure cabinets for critical devices. 
- Protect cabling and power supplies.

**Other Information** 
    Include environmental factors like heat or water damage.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Equipment is sometimes placed in high-risk areas (e.g., near windows or HVAC units).
- Cabling and power sources are often unprotected or exposed. 
- Organizations neglect environmental risks like overheating or water damage.
