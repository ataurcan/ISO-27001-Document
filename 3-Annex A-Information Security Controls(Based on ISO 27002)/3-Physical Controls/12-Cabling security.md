
**Control**
    Cables carrying power, data or supporting information services should be protected from interception, interference or damage.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203145440.png)


**Purpose** 
    To prevent loss, damage, theft or compromise of information and other associated assets and interruption to the organization's operations related to power and communications cabling.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Route cables to minimize risk of damage. 
- Use conduit or underground cabling where possible. 
- Label cables clearly and protect connection points.

**Other Information** 
    Regularly inspect cabling for tampering.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Cable routing is often done without security considerations. 
- Unlabeled or exposed cabling increases risk of tampering or damage.
- Inspection and maintenance of cabling is rarely scheduled or tracked.
