
**Control** 
    Premises should be continuously monitored for unauthorized physical access.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203130115.png)

**Purpose** 
    To detect and deter unauthorized physical access.

**Guidance (Summary)**
- Use CCTV, alarms and security patrols.
- Review monitoring logs regularly.
- Test monitoring systems to ensure effectiveness.

**Other Information** 
    Aligns with incident response planning.


**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Logs from CCTV and alarms are not consistently reviewed or retained. 
- Monitoring systems may lack regular testing or calibration. 
- Integration with incident response planning is frequently overlooked.
