
**Control** 
    Protection against physical and environmental threats, such as natural disasters and other intentional or unintentional physical threats to infrastructure should be designed and implemented.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203130548.png)


**Purpose** 
    To prevent or reduce the consequences of events originating from physical and environmental threats.

**Guidance (Summary)**
- Design buildings for resilience and safety.
- Install environmental controls like fire suppression. 
- Test and maintain protection systems.

**Other Information** 
    Part of business continuity planning

  
**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Many organizations underinvest in physical protections like fire suppression. 
- Environmental threats (e.g., water leaks, heat) are not always part of risk assessments. 
- Testing of protective systems is irregular or undocumented.
