
**Control** 
    Security perimeters should be defined and used to protect areas that contain information and other associated assets.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203123654.png)

**Purpose** 
    To prevent unauthorized physical access, damage and interference to the organization's information and other associated assets.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Establish secure areas with appropriate entry controls. 
- Use barriers, entry points and surveillance as needed.
- Review perimeter controls regularly.

**Other Information** 
    Design should consider threats like theft and environmental hazards.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Designing perimeters that balance security with operational access can be difficult. 
- Regular reviews of physical security layouts are often neglected. 
- Environmental threats are not always fully considered in design.
