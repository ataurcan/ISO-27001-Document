
**Control** 
    Clear desk rules for papers and removable storage media and clear screen rules for information processing facilities should be defined and appropriately enforced.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203131401.png)


**Purpose** 
    To reduce the risks of unauthorized access, loss of and damage to information on desks, screens and in other accessible locations during and outside normal working hours.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Require storage of papers and removable media when not in use. 
- Lock screens when unattended. 
- Train staff on clean desk practices.

**Other Information** 
    Reinforces physical and visual security.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Enforcement relies heavily on user behavior, making compliance inconsistent. 
- Lack of awareness or training leads to unattended sensitive documents/screens. 
- Visual security risks (e.g., shoulder surfing) are often underestimated.
