
**Control** 
    Physical security for offices, rooms and facilities should be designed and implemented.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203125219.png)


**Purpose** 
    To prevent unauthorized physical access, damage and interference to the organization's information and other associated assets in offices, rooms and facilities.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Lock doors and windows when unattended.
- Use alarms or surveillance for critical areas. 
- Restrict physical keys and access devices.


**Other Information** 
    Include work areas, server rooms and storage spaces.


**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Physical locking mechanisms are sometimes bypassed or not monitored. 
- Securing non-obvious areas like storage closets or meeting rooms is often missed. 
- Alarm and surveillance coverage may have blind spots.
