
**Control** 
    Records should be protected from loss, destruction, falsification, unauthorized access and unauthorized release.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203013518.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements, as well as community or societal expectations related to the protection and availability of records.

**Guidance (Summary)**
- Establish retention and protection policies. 
- Store records securely with proper access controls. 
- Ensure disposal methods prevent unauthorized recovery.

**Other Information** 
    Aligns with legal retention requirements.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Records are often stored without formal retention or deletion policies. 
- Secure storage and disposal practices are inconsistently applied across systems and formats. 
- Ensuring records meet both legal and operational needs is complex.
