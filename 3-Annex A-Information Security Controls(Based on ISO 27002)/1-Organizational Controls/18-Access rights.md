
**Control** 
    Access rights to information and other associated assets should be provisioned, reviewed, modified and removed in accordance with the organization's topic-specific policy on and rules for access control.


![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201115156.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure access to information and other associated assets is defined and authorized according to the business requirements.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use formal processes to approve and grant access. 
- Review access periodically, especially after role changes. 
- Revoke access promptly when no longer needed.

**Other Information** 
    Helps reduce insider threat.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Granting and revoking access is often informal or undocumented. 
- Inactive accounts or orphaned access often go unnoticed. 
- Monitoring of access rights changes is lacking in many organizations.
