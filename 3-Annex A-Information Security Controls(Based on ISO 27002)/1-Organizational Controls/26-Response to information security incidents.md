
**Control**
    Information security incidents should be responded to in accordance with the documented procedures.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202154441.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure efficient and effective response to information security incidents.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
-  Implement documented response procedures. 
- Coordinate with relevant stakeholders, including authorities if needed. 
- Contain, eradicate and recover from incidents.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Response processes are often not rehearsed or fully understood. 
- Coordination with external stakeholders is delayed due to unclear protocols. 
- Lessons learned are rarely integrated into the response process.
