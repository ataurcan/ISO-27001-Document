
**Control** 
    Information transfer rules, procedures, or agreements should be in place for all types of transfer facilities within the organization and between the organization and other parties.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201112405.png)


**Purpose** 
 To maintain the security of information transferred within an organization and with any external
 interested party.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use controls to prevent unauthorized access or loss. 
- Ensure traceability and chain of custody. 
- Apply encryption where needed.

**Other Information** 
    Covers electronic, physical and verbal transfers.


**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Many organizations lack formal procedures for verbal or informal data transfers. 
- Encryption and chain-of-custody are often not enforced consistently. 
- -Risk from shadow IT and uncontrolled sharing (e.g., personal email, USBs) is high.
