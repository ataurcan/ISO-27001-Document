
**Control**
    Knowledge gained from information security incidents should be used to strengthen and improve the information security controls.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202155736.png)

**Purpose** 
    To reduce the likelihood or consequences of future incidents.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Document incident findings and root causes. 
- Update controls and processes based on lessons learned. 
- Share lessons internally to raise awareness.

**Other Information** 
    Feeds continuous improvement.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Root cause analysis is skipped or incomplete due to lack of expertise. 
- Corrective actions are documented but not tracked or verified. 
- Lessons learned are not widely shared, reducing organizational awareness.
