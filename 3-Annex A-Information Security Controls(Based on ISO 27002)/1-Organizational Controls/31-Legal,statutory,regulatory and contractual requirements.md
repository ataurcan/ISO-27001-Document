
**Control** 
    Legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements relevant to information security and the organization's approach to meet these requirements should be identified, documented and kept up to date.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202223653.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to information security.


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Document all relevant legal and contractual security requirements. 
- Regularly review compliance and update obligations. 
- Communicate obligations to relevant parties.

**Other Information** 
    Non-compliance may result in legal penalties and reputational damage.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Organizations often have incomplete visibility of applicable legal and regulatory obligations, especially in multinational contexts. 
- Tracking changes in laws and ensuring timely updates to controls is challenging. 
- Legal requirements are not always effectively communicated to relevant departments.
