
**Control** 
    The organization should identify and meet the requirements regarding the preservation of privacy and protection of PIl according to applicable laws and regulations and contractual requirements.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203014004.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to the information security aspects of the protection of PII.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures. 
- Ensure processing is lawful, fair and transparent. 
- Support data subject rights and privacy impact assessments.

**Other Information** 
    Refer to ISO/IEC 27701 for detailed guidance.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Mapping and tracking PII across systems is difficult, especially in legacy environments.
- Organizations struggle to align with diverse regional privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA).
- Employees may lack awareness of data subject rights and privacy requirements.
