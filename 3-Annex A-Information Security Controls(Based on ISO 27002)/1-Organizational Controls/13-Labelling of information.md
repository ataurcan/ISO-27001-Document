
**Control** 
    An appropriate set of procedures for information labelling should be developed and implemented in accordance with the information classification scheme adopted by the organization.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201111931.png)

**Purpose** 
    To facilitate the communication of classification of information and support automation of information processing and management.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Label all formats of information. 
- Use physical labels, headers, footers or metadata. 
- Train staff on labelling procedures.

**Other Information** 
    Incorrect labelling can expose sensitive data.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Applying labels consistently across systems and formats (e.g., digital, paper) is difficult. 
- Users may not understand or follow labelling guidelines. 
- Mislabelling can lead to inappropriate access or data exposure.
