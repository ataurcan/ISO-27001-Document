
**Control** 
    The organization should assess information security events and decide if they are to be categorized as information security incidents.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202130352.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure effective categorization and prioritization of information security events.


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Establish criteria to identify events vs. incidents. 
- Assign responsibilities for event assessment. 
-  Log, track and communicate assessments.

**Other Information** 
    Accurate classification helps prioritize response.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Criteria to distinguish between events and incidents are often vague. 
- Event assessments are undocumented or handled informally. 
- Staff may escalate non-critical events or miss real threats due to lack of guidance.
