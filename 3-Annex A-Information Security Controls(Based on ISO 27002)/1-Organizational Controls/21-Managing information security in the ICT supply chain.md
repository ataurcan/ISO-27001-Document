
**Control** 
    Processes and procedures should be defined and implemented to manage the information security risks associated with the ICT products and services supply chain.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201124313.png)

**Purpose** 
    To maintain an agreed level of information security in supplier relationships.


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Identify and assess ICT supply chain risks. 
- Include security requirements in supplier contracts and SLAs. 
- Monitor supplier compliance and changes that may affect security.

**Other Information** 
    Aligns with risk treatment and continuity plans.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Organizations often overlook supplier risk assessments beyond onboarding. 
- Ensuring enforcement of security clauses across all tiers in the supply chain is difficult. 
- Monitoring suppliers for compliance and changes is resource-intensive.
