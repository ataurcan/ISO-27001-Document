
**Control** 
    Rules to control physical and logical access to information and other associated assets should be established and implemented based on business and information security requirements.


![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201113308.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure authorized access and to prevent unauthorized access to information and other associated assets.

**Guidance (Summary)**  
- Create an access control policy. 
- Enforce least privilege and need-to-know principles. 
- Review access rights regularly.

**Other Information** 
    Access control must match classification levels.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Least privilege principles are often bypassed for convenience or speed. 
- Periodic access reviews are neglected or not systematized. 
- Access control rules often don't align with data classification schemes.
