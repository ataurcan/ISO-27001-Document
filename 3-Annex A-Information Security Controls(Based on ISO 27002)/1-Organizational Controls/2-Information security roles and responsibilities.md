**Control**
    Information security roles and responsibilities should be defined and allocated according to the organization needs.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260130121325.png)


**Purpose** 
    To establish a defined, approved and understood structure for the implementation, operation and management of information security within the organization.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Define responsibilities for protecting information assets and managing risks.
- Include responsibilities for users, processes and risk acceptance. 
- Document and communicate responsibilities clearly.
- Assign competent personnel and ensure up-to-date skills.

**Other Information**
    Organizations often appoint an information security manager; asset owners remain responsible.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
    Difficulty distinguishing between overarching policy and topic-specific policies.
