**Control** 
    The organization should establish and maintain contact with special interest groups or other specialist security forums and professional associations.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260130124521.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure appropriate flow of information takes place with respect to information security.

**Guidance (Summary)**
- Gain insights on best practices and threat intelligence. 
- Receive alerts and specialist advice. 
- Build liaison points for incident management.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Participation is deprioritized due to perceived low value.
- Evaluating trustworthiness and relevance of groups is difficult. 
- Shared intelligence often isn't integrated into ISMS practices.
