
**Control** 
    An  inventory of information and other associated assets, including owners, should be developed and maintained.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201105228.png)

**Purpose** 
    To identify the organization's information and other associated assets in order to preserve their information security and assign appropriate ownership.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Keep inventories accurate and updated 
- Include location and classification details. 
- Assign and document asset ownership with clear responsibilities.

**Other Information** 
    Supports risk management, audit, incident response.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Keeping inventory updated is difficult, especially in dynamic IT environments. 
- Ownership and classification of non-physical assets are often unclear. 
- Integrating inventories with other systems is resource-intensive.
