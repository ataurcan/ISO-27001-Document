
**Control** 
    The organization should plan how to maintain information security at an appropriate level during disruption.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202221023.png)


**Purpose** 
    To protect information and other associated assets during disruption.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Integrate security into business continuity planning. 
- Identify critical assets and minimum-security requirements.
- Test security measures under disruption scenarios.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Security controls are not always incorporated into business continuity plans. 
- Disruption scenarios are rarely tested with a security lens. 
- Identifying minimum security levels for critical assets is often skipped.
