
**Control** 
    Operating procedures for information processing facilities should be documented and made available to personnel who need them.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203024533.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure the correct and secure operation of information processing facilities.

Guidance (Summary) 
- Include procedures for secure system operation and maintenance. 
-  Update documents to reflect system changes.
- Communicate procedures to relevant personnel.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Operating procedures are outdated or incomplete, especially during rapid system changes. 
- Procedures are not always accessible or communicated to relevant users. 
- Lack of version control and review mechanisms results in inconsistencies.
