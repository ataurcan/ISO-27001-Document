**Control** 
    The organization should establish and maintain contact with relevant authorities.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260130123716.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure appropriate flow of information takes place with respect to information security between the organization and relevant legal, regulatory and supervisory authorities.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Specify when and by whom authorities should be contacted. 
- Define reporting procedures for incidents. 
- Maintain contacts for understanding legal and regulatory expectations.


**Other Information**
    Useful for incident management and compliance.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Confusion over which authority to contact for which incidents. 
- Fear of legal/reputational impact may delay reporting. 
- Procedures for legal and regulatory liaison are often unclear or outdated.
