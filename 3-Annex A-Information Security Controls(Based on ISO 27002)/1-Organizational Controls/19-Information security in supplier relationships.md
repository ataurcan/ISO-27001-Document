
**Control** 
    Processes and procedures should be defined and implemented to manage the information security risks associated with the use of supplier's products or services.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201120038.png)

**Purpose** 
    To maintain an agreed level of information security in supplier relationships.


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Assess suppliers for security risks. 
- Include security clauses in contracts.
- Monitor suppliers' compliance with requirements.

**Other Information** 
     Cloud services and outsourced providers.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Supplier risk assessments are skipped or treated as one-time activities.
- Security clauses are often generic or missing from contracts. 
- Continuous monitoring of supplier compliance is rarely performed.
