
**Control** 
    The organization should regularly monitor, review, evaluate and manage change in supplier information security practices and service delivery.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201144628.png)

**Purpose** 
    To maintain an agreed level of information security and service delivery in line with supplier agreements.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Review supplier performance and security controls. 
- Monitor changes to supplier services and assess their impact.
- Update agreements when changes affect security requirements.

**Other Information** 
    Supports risk management and continuity of secure operations.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
  -Tracking changes in outsourced services that affect security is often uncoordinated.
  -Many organizations lack formal review mechanisms or KPls for supplier performance. 
  -Supplier contract updates lag behind service modifications.
