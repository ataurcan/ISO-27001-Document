
**Control** 
    Rules for the acceptable use and procedures for handling information and other associated assets should be identified, documented and implemented.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201110120.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure information and other associated assets are appropriately protected, used and handled.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Establish a topic-specific acceptable use policy. 
- Define permitted and prohibited behaviors. 
- Include procedures for handling assets through their lifecycle.

**Other Information** 
    Should cover cloud services and collaboration environments as well.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Policies struggle to keep up with new tech like BYOD and cloud services. 
- Enforcement is challenging without impacting user privacy. 
- Coverage for third parties is often overlooked.
