
**Control** 
    The organization should implement appropriate procedures to protect intellectual property rights.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202224333.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to  intellectual property rights and use of proprietary products.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use software legally and manage licenses properly.
-  Protect organization's own IP through appropriate controls. 
- Monitor compliance with IP agreements.

**Other Information** 
    Includes patents, copyrights, trademarks, and trade secrets.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Ensuring legal use and licensing of third-party software is frequently overlooked. 
- Protection of organizational IP is weak due to lack of classification or access control
- Monitoring IP compliance across departments and partners is rarely enforced.
