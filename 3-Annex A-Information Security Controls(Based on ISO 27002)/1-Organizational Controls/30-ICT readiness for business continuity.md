
**Control** 
    ICT readiness should be planned, implemented, maintained and tested based on business continuity objectives and ICT continuity requirements.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202223120.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure the availability of the organization's information and other associated assets during disruption.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Develop ICT continuity plans aligned with business priorities. 
-  Test and update ICT recovery procedures. 
- Coordinate ICT readiness with overall continuity management.

**Other Information** 
     Aligns with ISO/IEC 27031 guidance.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- ICT continuity plans often lack alignment with business continuity strategy. 
- Testing of ICT recovery procedures is not performed regularly or realistically.
- Coordination between ICT and other departments is weak during disruptions.
