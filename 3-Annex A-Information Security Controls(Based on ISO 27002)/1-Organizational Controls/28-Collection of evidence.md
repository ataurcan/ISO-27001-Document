
**Control** 
    The organization should establish and implement procedures for the identification, collection, acquisition and preservation of evidence related to information security events.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260202220446.png)


**Purpose**
    To ensure a consistent and effective management of evidence related to information security incidents for the purposes of disciplinary and legal actions.

Guidance (Summary) 
- Follow a defined chain of custody. 
- Use reliable logging and monitoring tools. 
- Secure and restrict access to collected evidence.

**Other Information** 
    Seek legal advice for evidence handling.


**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Chain of custody procedures are often undocumented or improperly followed. 
- Staff may not know how to preserve evidence, compromising legal usability.
- Evidence storage and access control measures are frequently insufficient.
