
**Control** 
    Compliance with the organization's information security policy, topic-specific policies, rules and standards should be regularly reviewed.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203023919.png)


**Purpose** 
    To ensure that information security is implemented and operated in accordance with the organization's information security policy, topic-specific policies, rules and standards.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Regularly check compliance through audits and inspections. 
- Document and address non-conformities. 
- Take corrective actions as needed.

**Other Information** 
    Non-compliance should be escalated and tracked.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Audits and inspections are irregular or only done for certification purposes.
- Non-conformities are not always documented or followed up with corrective actions.
- Enforcement is weak where accountability for violations is unclear.
