
**Control** 
    The organization should plan and prepare for managing information security incidents by defining, establishing and communicating information security incident management processes, roles and responsibilities.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260201150654.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure quick, effective, consistent and orderly response to information security incidents, including communication on information security events.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Develop and communicate an incident management policy and procedures. 
- Define roles, responsibilities and escalation paths. 
- Test and update the incident management plan regularly.

**Other Information** 
    Align with legal requirements and reporting obligations.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Incident management plans often exist but are not tested or updated. 
- Roles and escalation paths are not well defined or communicated. 
- Plans may not meet legal or regulatory reporting timelines.
