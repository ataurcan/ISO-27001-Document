**Control** 
    Information relating to information security threats should be collected and analysed to produce threat intelligence.
    
![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260130132900.png)

**Purpose** 
    To provide awareness of the organization's threat environment so that the appropriate mitigation actions can be taken.

Guidance (Summary)
- Gather strategic, tactical and operational threat information. 
- Use threat intelligence to improve prevention, detection and response.
- Share threat intelligence with trusted partners.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Lack of in-house expertise to collect and interpret threat intelligence. 
- Confusion about types (strategic/tactical) and how to act on them. 
- Integration with response or risk processes is often weak or siloed.
