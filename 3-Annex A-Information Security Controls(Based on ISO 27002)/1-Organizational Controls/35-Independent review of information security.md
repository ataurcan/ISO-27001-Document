
**Control** 
    The organization's approach to managing information security and its implementation including people, processes and technologies should be reviewed independently at planned intervals, or when significant changes occur.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203023159.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure the continuing suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the organization's approach to managing information security

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Engage independent reviewers (internal or external). 
- Assess policy implementation, controls and risk treatment. 
- Report findings to top management and act on recommendations.

**Other Information** 
    Supports continual improvement.


Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application 
- Independent reviews are sometimes skipped or performed superficially. 
- Internal reviews may lack objectivity or technical expertise.
- Findings from reviews are often delayed or not acted upon systematically.
