
**Control** 
    The organization should provide a mechanism for personnel to report observed or suspected information security events through appropriate channels in a timely manner.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203122935.png)

**Purpose**
    To support timely, consistent and effective reporting of information security events that can be identified by personnel. 

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Define clear reporting channels and responsibilities. 
- Encourage reporting without fear of retaliation. 
- Provide training on what and how to report.

**Other Information** 
    Feeds into incident management process.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Employees may hesitate to report due to fear of blame or unclear procedures. 
- Reporting channels are often not visible, trusted, or well-communicated. 
- Lack of training leads to underreporting or misidentification of events.
