
**Control** 
    Personnel of the organization and relevant interested parties should receive appropriate information security awareness, education and training and regular updates of the organization's information security policy, topic-specific policies and procedures, as relevant for their job function.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203030657.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure personnel and relevant interested parties are aware of and fulfl their information security responsibilities.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Run induction and refresher training programs. 
- Tailor training to roles and responsibilities. 
- Raise awareness of threats like phishing and social engineering.

**Other Information** 
    Monitor effectiveness and update content regularly.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Training is often treated as a checkbox activity, lacking customisation per role.
- Measuring training effectiveness is difficult without clear metrics. 
- Updates to content lag behind emerging threats (e.g., phishing techniques).
