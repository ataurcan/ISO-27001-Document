
**Control** 
    Confidentiality or non-disclosure agreements reflecting the organization's needs for the protection of information should be identified, documented, regularly reviewed and signed by personnel and other relevant interested parties.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203112454.png)


**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Use NDAs for employees, contractors and suppliers. 
- Review NDAs periodically for adequacy.
- Clarify terms, duration and enforcement of confidentiality.

**Other Information** 
    Supports legal action if confidentiality is breached.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- NDAs may be inconsistently applied across departments or roles. 
- NDA clauses are often outdated or not aligned with evolving risks. 
- Enforcement of NDAs post-employment is rarely monitored or followed up.
