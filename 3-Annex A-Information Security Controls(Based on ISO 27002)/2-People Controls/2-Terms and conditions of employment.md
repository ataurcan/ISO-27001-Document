
**Control** 
    The employment contractual agreements should state the personnel's and the organization's responsibilities for information security.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203030016.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure personnel understand their information security responsibilities for the roles for which they are considered

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Include confidentiality, acceptable use and security responsibilities in contracts. 
- Clearly define consequences for violations. 
- Ensure staff understand terms before starting work.

**Other Information** 
    Covers contractors and third-party personnel too.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
-  Information security clauses are often generic or missing in employment contracts. 
- Ensuring understanding and acknowledgment of obligations before onboarding is often skipped. 
- Temporary or third-party staff may fall outside formal HR agreements.
