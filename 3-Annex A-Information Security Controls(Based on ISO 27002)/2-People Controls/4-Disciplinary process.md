
**Control** 
    A disciplinary process should be formalized and communicated to take actions against personnel and other relevant interested parties who have committed an information security policy violation.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203031132.png)

**Purpose** 
    To ensure personnel and other relevant interested parties understand the consequences of information security policy violation, to deter and appropriately deal with personnel and other relevant interested parties who committed the violation.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
- Define fair and clear disciplinary procedures. 
- Apply proportionate measures for violations. 
- Communicate the process to all staff.

**Other Information** 
    Ensure compliance with employment laws.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application** 
- Disciplinary procedures are vague or not directly linked to security breaches. 
- Fear of legal implications can lead to hesitation in enforcing consequences. 
- Communication of the process to all employees is often incomplete or unclear.
