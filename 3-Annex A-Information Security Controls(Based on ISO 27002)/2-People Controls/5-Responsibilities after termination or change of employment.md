
**Control**
    Information security responsibilities and duties that remain valid after termination or change of employment should be defined, enforced and communicated to relevant personnel and other interested parties.

![](../../PNGs/Pasted%20image%2020260203031700.png)


**Purpose** 
    To protect the organization's interests as part of the process of changing or terminating employment or contracts.

**Guidance (Summary)** 
     -Remind individuals of ongoing confidentiality obligations.
     - Retrieve access rights, assets and credentials.
     - Provide guidance for returning confidential data.

**Other Information** 
    Covers permanent and temporary staff.

**Difficulties in Control Interpretation and Application**
- Delays in revoking access rights or collecting assets increase security risks. 
- Ongoing confidentiality obligations are rarely reinforced at offboarding. 
- Tracking return of confidential data across devices/cloud is often weak.
