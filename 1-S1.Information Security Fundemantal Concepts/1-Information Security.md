
Conditions or factors that affect, or could affect, the **confidentiality**,
**integrity**, or **availability** of information within the context of the
organization, including those that may impact interested parties such
as employees, customers, suppliers, regulators, and other relevant
stakeholders.

**What is ISO 27001 and its Objectives:** 
1. Ensure Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (CIA Triad)
2. Establish a Risk-Based Information Security Management System (ISMS)
3. Ensure Legal, Regulatory, and Contractual Compliance 
4. Enable Continual Improvement in Information Security
5. Enhance Stakeholder Confidence and Business Reputation 
6. Foster a Culture of Information Security Awareness


**Information Security Management System (ISMS):**
A set of interrelated or interacting elements of an organization used to establish policies, objectives, and processes to manage risks to information security and to achieve the preservation of confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
