The PDCA concept is an iterative process used by organizations to achieve continual improvement.

The PDCA can be applied to a management system and to each of its individual elements, as follows:

a) **Plan:** Determine and assess information security risks and opportunities establish information security objectives, and define the processes anc controls necessary to achieve results in line with the organization's information security policy.
b) **Do**: Implement the information security processes, controls, anc procedures as planned to mitigate risks and meet objectives.
c) **Check**: Monitor, measure, and evaluate the effectiveness of information security activities and controls against the policy, objectives, and risk criteria; report on performance and compliance. 
d) **Act**: Take corrective actions and apply continual improvement practices to how pdCA applies to an information security intended outcomes for  information security.
