**Clause 1- Scope**

This clause Covers;
 • Purpose of the Standard 
 • Risk Focus
 • Applicability of the Standard 
 • Mandatory Clauses

**Purpose of the Standard:** 
The standard defines requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS) within the context of an organization.

**Risk Focus**:
It includes requirements for the assessment and treatment of information security risks,tailored to the organization's needs.

**Applicability of the Standard:**
The requirements are generic and applicable to all organizations regardless of type, size, or industry..

**Mandatory Clauses:** 
Excluding any requirements from Clauses 4 to 10 is not acceptable if an organization claims conformity with the standard.


**Clause 2- Normative References** 

The normative reference of ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Is;
ISO/IEC 27000: Information technology - Security techniques - Information security management systems - Overview and vocabulary.


**Clause 3- Terms and definitions** 

Importance of the definitions: 
1. Establish a Common Understanding of Key Concepts.
2. Ensures Clarity in implementing the Standard 
3. Promotes consistency across organizations 
4. Provide reference points for interpreting requirements.
